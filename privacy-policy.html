We do not sell personal information. We may share limited information with hosting, email, security, professional, or technology service providers when needed to operate the website or respond to you. We may also disclose information when required by law or to protect rights and safety.

We retain enquiry information only as long as reasonably needed for communication, service delivery, recordkeeping, dispute resolution, and legal compliance. We use reasonable administrative and technical safeguards, but no online transmission or storage method is completely secure.

Subject to applicable Indian law, you may ask us to correct or delete information you submitted, withdraw consent for future use, or raise a privacy concern. Some information may need to be retained where required by law or for legitimate business records.
