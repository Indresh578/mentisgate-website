About MentisGate

Where education meets practical innovation.

MentisGate Learning Private Limited is an education-focused technology and services company helping institutions modernize operations, strengthen recruitment, and improve learning experiences.

We work with schools, colleges, universities, coaching institutes, NGOs, training institutes, skill development centers, educational foundations, and educational organizations across India.

Our mission

Bridge the gap between education, technology, employability, and institutional growth.

Founder & Company Recognition

WILSA Award 2025

Award for Contribution to Education Community.

Founder & Director Indresh Arora was recognized for work in educational innovation, teacher empowerment, recruitment, skill development, and technology-driven transformation.

Through MentisGate Learning and TeacherJob.in, he is building practical solutions that help educational institutions recruit better, automate operations, and grow sustainably.

The honor was conferred in the presence of distinguished leaders including Dr. A. S. Kiran Kumar, former Chairman of ISRO.

“This recognition motivates us to continue creating impact at a larger scale.”Indresh Arora, Founder & Director
