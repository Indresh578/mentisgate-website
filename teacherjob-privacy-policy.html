Privacy Policy

Last updated: 20 June 2026

TeacherJob.in is an educational recruitment and employment platform operated by MentisGate Learning Private Limited for candidates, educational institutions, recruiters, employers, and job seekers.

1. Information we collect

We may collect information such as name, mobile number, email address, address, educational qualifications, professional experience, resume or CV, profile photo, employment preferences, skills, certifications, account activity, institution or organization details, contact person details, job posting information, recruitment information, and verification information where required.

We may also collect device information, browser information, IP address, login records, usage information, application version, cookies, and similar technologies.

2. Purpose of collection

Information is used to create and manage accounts, enable recruitment services, match candidates with employers, verify users and institutions, process subscriptions and payments, improve platform performance, prevent fraud and misuse, comply with legal obligations, and provide customer support.

3. Communications

Users may receive job alerts, recruitment updates, service notifications, promotional offers, educational opportunities, and subscription information through email, SMS, WhatsApp, push notifications, phone calls, or similar channels. Users may opt out of marketing communications where applicable.

4. Data sharing

Information may be shared with schools, colleges, universities, coaching institutes, educational organizations, recruiters, employers, and hiring partners for recruitment, verification, hiring, and employment-related purposes. Information may also be shared with cloud service providers, analytics providers, payment processors, and legal authorities where required by law.

5. Verification information

TeacherJob.in may provide optional verification programs for candidates, employers, recruiters, schools, colleges, universities, coaching institutes, and other organizations. Users who apply may be asked for identity, organizational, educational, or supporting documents. Verification documents are used for identity verification, fraud prevention, platform security, trust and safety measures, legal compliance, and related operational purposes.

6. Paid services and retention

TeacherJob.in may offer premium memberships, subscription plans, featured listings, paid job postings, and promotional services. Payment-related information may be processed through authorized payment service providers. Information is retained as long as reasonably necessary to provide services, maintain records, resolve disputes, enforce agreements, and comply with legal obligations.

7. Security and user rights

Reasonable technical, administrative, and organizational safeguards are used to protect personal information, though no electronic system can guarantee absolute security. Subject to applicable law, users may request access, updates, corrections, account closure, or withdrawal of consent where applicable.

8. Third-party services and children

TeacherJob.in may contain links to third-party websites and services. We are not responsible for third-party privacy practices. The platform is intended only for users legally eligible to seek employment or represent organizations.
